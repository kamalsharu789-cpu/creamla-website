Our Story

Born From Passion,
Crafted With Purity

CreAm'la was created to redefine premium ice cream by using rich buffalo milk — known for its naturally higher fat content, superior creaminess, and dense texture — as the heart of every scoop.

We combine this liquid gold with real fruits, natural flavours, and pure ingredients to deliver an ice cream experience that is as close to nature as possible. No shortcuts. No compromise.

Our mission is simple: give you ice cream that is denser, richer, and more authentic than anything you've ever tasted — without a single artificial additive.

The Premium Difference

Richer. Denser. Better.

Regular ice cream uses cow milk or reconstituted milk powder. CreAm'la uses pure buffalo milk — which contains nearly double the fat content of cow milk, creating a naturally thicker, denser, more satisfying scoop.
